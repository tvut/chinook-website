Remindr

When you can't bring yourself to do work

Remindr is designed to help high school students who face the daily struggle of not being able to work productively at home.

Start working earlier

Remindr helps you set timeframes and keeps you accountable for sticking to them, creating better study habits.

Get more done quicker

Remindr negates blocking factors such as your phone and provides positive peer pressure to motivate you to work.

Keep yourself motivated

Remindr works to create long term study habits but will continuously support you if necessary.

How it works

1 Adding Friends

Remindr is based around the idea of working with people you know. Remindr lets you connect with these people by adding them as friends, both from your contacts and by username.

After you've added your friends you will be able to see who is currently working and what they are working on. If they are not paired with a productivity partner Remindr will give you the option to join with them.

2 Scheduling Time

At the beginning of everyday, Remindr will ask you when you want to do work during that day and for how long. Remindr will also take into account what work you want to do and for how long.

As you use the app more, Remindr will begin to learn from your previous work sessions and provide suggestions for time blocks that are optimized to let you do the most with your time.

3 Initiating Work

Through our testing, we have found that it takes more than just a simple notification to get people off the couch and to their desk. Remindr simulates a phone call, hijacking your phone and demanding immediate action. Remindr has no decline option, you can either delay your work session for a short period of time or accept the call. In order to cancel your call you must go directly to the app and cancel your time session.

Accepting the notification takes you straight to a video call within our app.

4 Work Time

Now that you're back in the app, Remindr connects you to someone in your friends work who is also trying to do work. If no one else is available, the app will pull up a recording from a previous session of one of your friends working.

Remindr is designed to provide a productive experience and will automatically mute parties depending on ambient noise and whether it detects off topic discussion. The app also prevents off topic phone usage while on the call.

At the end of your allotted time block, Remindr will automatically end the call, freeing you and your phone to take a break.
